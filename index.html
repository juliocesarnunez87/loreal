Service

Our company Loreal Tecnic C.A Provides services for laboratory technology in production processes, food and fodder.

We are oriented to the following:

  • Equipment for sample preparation, Mills, Distillers, Digesters.
  • UV visible spectrophotometers, NIRS (Near Infrared) and FTIR.
  • Chromatography equipment, HPLC.
  • TOC
  • Viscometers, Densimeters, Rheometers.
  • pH meters, KF, Titrators, Turbidimeters, Conductometers, Colorimeters.
  • Analytical Balances, Precision and Humidity Balances.
  • Extraction hoods.

We have qualified technicians for the main brands, with years of experience in the area.
